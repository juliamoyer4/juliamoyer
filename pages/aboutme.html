About Me

Hello! My name is Julia Moyer and I am a Junior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am studying Consumer Behavior and Marketplace Studies and Information Science. I am interested in the intersection of design, technology, and consumers.

I love spending time playing video games with friends, going to Badger sporting events, and baking! My favorite sporting events include Badger Football games, Badger Basketball, and Badger Volleyball sporting events!

I am involved in two organizations on campus. I am a part of the Student Retail Association (SRA) and the Girls Who Code club.

I am from Racine, Wisconsin, and back home I have three cats! My cats' names are: Tuxedo, Flora, and Hailey.
